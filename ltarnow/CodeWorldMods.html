Response to Statement

There are many applications online which use noise-generated terrain land forms, particularly 2D and 3D games. Most of them are extremely sophisticated and use biome generation and are too sophisticated for the project we will probably build. However most of these generators spark good ideas in my mind. A polygonal map generation tool seems like the correct way to go in terms of creating randomly generated worlds. The worlds generated have areas of land-mass that stick together instead of being sporadically spread around. In terms of our 2D world, a great way to randomly create structured land masses would be polygonal map generation.
